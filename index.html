Dream, Grow.>

Amanda Swan is a visionary children's author whose stories transport young readers into enchanting worlds.

"Books have the power to whisk us away on magical adventures, ignite our imaginations, and sow seeds of wonder in young hearts."

Amanda Swan is a celebrated author specializing in children's literature, known for her enchanting stories that captivate young imaginations. Born on June 8, 1980, in a quaint town on the East Coast, Amanda’s love for writing blossomed during her childhood adventures in nature.

Amanda Swan

In addition to her writing, Amanda is an advocate for childhood literacy and regularly conducts interactive storytelling sessions in schools and libraries across the country.

Education and Experience.

biography

Amanda's profound education in literature has greatly influenced her exceptional storytelling and captivating writing style.

1998-2002

Bachelor’s Degree in English Literature from Yale University

During her studies at Yale University, Amanda Swan deepened her understanding of literary genres and expressive writing.

2003–2005

Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Writing for Children from Vermont College

Amanda refined her storytelling skills, specializing in crafting engaging narratives that resonate with young readers.

2007

Publication of “The Magical Treehouse Adventures,” debut book

The publication of her debut book marked a significant milestone in Amanda Swan’s career, establishing her as a talented children's author.
